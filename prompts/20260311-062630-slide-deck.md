## Key decisions

**Root cause of faded elements**: The Playwright screenshot wait time was only
800ms between slides, but CSS animations with `animation-fill-mode: both` kept
elements at `opacity: 0` until their delay expired. `.anim-4` had a 0.7s delay
and `.anim-5` had 0.9s delay, meaning they were still mid-fade or invisible
when screenshots were taken. This caused slide 2's second blockquote, slide 6's
pill badges, slide 10's blockquote, slide 11's bottom stats, slide 16's
secondary stats row, and slide 21's pill badges to appear nearly invisible.

**Fix 1 — Playwright wait time**: Increased initial wait from 1000ms to 1800ms
and between-slide wait from 800ms to 1500ms. This ensures all animations
complete before screenshot capture.

**Fix 2 — Shorter animation delays**: Reduced animation delays from
(0.1s, 0.3s, 0.5s, 0.7s, 0.9s) to (0.05s, 0.2s, 0.35s, 0.5s, 0.65s). The
last element now completes at 0.65s + 0.6s = 1.25s, well within the 1.5s
between-slide wait. This also improves the presentation feel — content appears
slightly snappier without losing the staggered entrance effect.

**Fix 3 — Card contrast**: Changed `.card` background from
`rgba(255,255,255,0.9)` to solid `#FFFFFF` and increased box-shadow from
`rgba(0,0,0,0.06)` to `rgba(0,0,0,0.10)`. On tinted slide backgrounds
(amber `#FFF8EF`, blue `#EBF3FF`, purple `#F3EEFF`) the semi-transparent
background blended into the slide color, reducing card visibility. Solid white
with stronger shadow ensures cards pop clearly on all backgrounds.

**What looked good already**: All slides had correct light CSS themes
(`.slide-hero`, `.slide-blue`, `.slide-amber`, `.slide-purple` all using light
backgrounds with dark `#1A2332` text). Charts had dark axis tick colors
`rgba(0,0,0,0.45)`. No dark backgrounds were present — the main issue was
purely animation timing in screenshot capture.
